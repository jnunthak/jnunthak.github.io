About Me

Hi, I'm Jeyadave Nuntha Kumar, a passionate Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) engineer and researcher with a deep interest in aerodynamics, turbulence modeling, and simulation techniques. I thrive at the intersection of technical rigor and creative problem-solving, focusing on impactful projects that push the boundaries of aerospace technology.

I'm currently completing my undergraduate studies in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Maryland - College Park, with minors in Mathematics and Global Engineering Leadership. My academic journey is driven by a commitment to excellence, reflected in my consistent academic achievements and involvement in cutting-edge research.

What Drives Me

My professional interests are rooted in making significant contributions to the aerospace industry, particularly through the lens of CFD. Beyond engineering, I am passionate about fostering inclusivity in STEM, mentoring aspiring engineers, and bridging the gap between academia and real-world applications.

Outside of Work
